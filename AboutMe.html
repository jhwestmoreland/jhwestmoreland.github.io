IOT-Based Smart Street Canopy Lighting

  • View

Team WACC 2018-2019

This project was for our Senior Design class. We were sponsored by Intelativ to re-design, from scratch, their "IOT-Based Smart Street Canopy Lighting Mark 1" and add their new requirements. The new requirements include: converting the project to wifi instead of ethernet wired, a NOIR Camera, an extra temperature sensor, decrease total cost.

WACC Requirements

Utilizing the agile method, weekly updates, and well documenting the project. We successfully created the device on time and I was able to successfully demonstrate it to our sponsor.
